**Purpose:** Define the ideal customers for the CommissionCrowd Invisible Agent — a headless, invisible automation system that handles lead research, hyper-personalized email drafting, approval, and triggered outreach inside Google Sheets and Telegram.

---

### 1. Overview

The **CommissionCrowd Invisible Agent** is a done-for-you pipeline automation service for B2B businesses that want consistent, high-quality outbound leads and personalized outreach **without** adopting new software, learning complex tools, or managing another dashboard.

It targets time-poor or non-technical business owners and sales leaders who:
- Already work in familiar tools (Google Sheets, email, or simple CRMs)
- Need reliable pipeline but dislike (or cannot justify) the cost and complexity of tools like Clay, Apollo, Instantly, or Lemlist
- Value **outcomes** (meetings booked, pipeline filled) over technology

The product wins by being **invisible** — results appear in the client’s existing workflow with only light human oversight via a shared Google Sheet and Telegram notifications.

|-----------------------|------------------------------------|--------------------------------|------------------------------|
| **Company Size**      | 5–50 employees                     | 1–5 or 50–100                  | Enterprise (>100)            |

| **Location**          | South Africa (Gauteng, Western Cape) + English-speaking markets (US, UK, Australia, Canada) | Other English-speaking regions | Non-English primary markets  |
| **Sales Model**       | Outbound / warm outreach heavy     | Mix of inbound + outbound      | Pure inbound or channel sales |

#### Technographics & Tool Stack
- Currently uses or is comfortable with: **Google Sheets**, Gmail/Google Workspace, basic CRMs (Pipedrive, HubSpot free tier, or none)
- Has **tried or rejected** complex sales tools because of complexity, cost, or “another login” fatigue
- Low technical sophistication or limited time to manage automation themselves
- Open to giving access to Google Sheets or email for automation purposes

#### Behavioral & Psychographic Profile
- **Hates new software** and “shiny object” tools
- Values **simplicity and reliability** over features
- Willing to pay for **results** rather than build or manage systems themselves
- Time-poor: wears multiple hats (founder + sales + operations)
- Has tried (or is currently doing) manual outreach or basic templates and is frustrated with inconsistency or low response rates
- Prefers **done-for-you** or “managed service” models over self-serve SaaS
- Appreciates human oversight and quality control (doesn’t want fully autonomous AI spam)

#### Explicit Exclusions (Who We Do NOT Target)
- Companies that already have sophisticated sales tech stacks and dedicated SDRs
- Businesses looking for a self-serve dashboard or full self-managed automation platform
- High-compliance industries (healthcare, finance, legal) without strong internal compliance support
- Companies unwilling to share lead lists or give light access to Sheets/email
- Purely inbound or product-led growth businesses with no outbound motion

### 3. Primary Buyer Personas

#### Persona 1: The Overwhelmed Founder / Owner-Operator (Primary)
- **Role:** Founder, CEO, or Managing Director
- **Age:** 30–50
- **Company:** Trades/service business (HVAC, plumbing, electrical, construction-related) or small agency
- **Pain:** Spends evenings/weekends doing sales outreach or has inconsistent pipeline because they’re too busy delivering work
- **Quote:** “I know I need to be reaching out consistently, but I don’t have time to research and write good emails every day — and I don’t want another dashboard to log into.”
- **Decision Criteria:** Simplicity, trust in the operator, clear ROI on meetings/pipeline, minimal time commitment from them

#### Persona 2: The Hands-On Sales Leader
- **Role:** Sales Director, Head of Growth, or Founder who still sells
- **Company:** Digital/marketing agency, recruitment agency, or B2B service business
- **Pain:** Has a small team or is solo and needs consistent top-of-funnel activity without hiring another full-time person
- **Quote:** “I want pipeline every week without having to manage another tool or teach my team a new system.”
- **Decision Criteria:** Quality of personalization, reliability of delivery, ability to review/approve easily

#### Persona 3: The Growth-Focused Micro-Agency Owner
- **Role:** Agency founder scaling client acquisition
- **Pain:** Needs predictable client acquisition for their own business but doesn’t want to build or manage complex outbound infrastructure
- **Quote:** “I sell this kind of service to my clients — I should probably be using something similar for myself.”

### 9. Go-to-Market & Prioritization Notes

**Primary Target (First 3–6 months):**
- South African B2B service businesses (HVAC, trades, agencies) in Gauteng and Western Cape — easier sales, same time zone, lower friction pilots.

**Secondary / Scale Target:**
- English-speaking international clients (US, UK, Australia, Canada) — higher willingness to pay once proof exists.

**Acquisition Channels (aligned with product nature):**
- Direct outreach using the system itself (ironic but effective)
- Referrals from early clients
- Content showing “before/after” pipeline results (without revealing tech)
- Partnerships with accountants, business coaches, or existing agencies serving these segments

**Pricing Sweet Spot for ICP:**
- Pilots: $300–$750
- Monthly retainers: $800 – $2,000 (most common range for this profile)
