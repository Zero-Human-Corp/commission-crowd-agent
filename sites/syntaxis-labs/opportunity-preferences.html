Commission Structure Preferences

✅ Preferred

  • Recurring or residual commission (subscription/retainer models)
  • Transparent payout terms with clear reporting
  • Monthly or quarterly payment cadence
  • Clear commission percentage (typically 10–30%)
  • No hidden clawbacks or delayed payouts
  • Sales enablement and training provided

❌ Not Preferred

  • One-time-only commission with no recurring potential
  • Opaque payout structures or unclear terms
  • Excessive exclusivity requirements (same-category)
  • Punitive non-compete clauses
  • Unrealistic quota requirements
