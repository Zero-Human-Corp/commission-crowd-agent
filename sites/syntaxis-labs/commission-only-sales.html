Commission-Only Sales

How we work, what we offer, and why commission-only aligns incentives.

What Is Commission-Only Sales?

Commission-only sales means we earn money only when we generate results for our partners. There are no retainers, no upfront fees, and no guaranteed minimums. We invest our time in prospecting, outreach, and qualification — and we get paid a percentage of the deals we help originate.

This model aligns our incentives perfectly with yours: we only succeed when you succeed.

Our Sales Process

1. ICP Definition

We work with you to define your ideal customer profile: company size, industry, geography, pain points, and buying triggers.

2. Lead Sourcing

We identify and research prospects that match your ICP using public data, compliant databases, and targeted search.

3. Outreach & Engagement

We initiate contact through email, LinkedIn, and other compliant channels — always with a clear value proposition.

4. Appointment Setting

Our primary strength is securing meetings and demos with qualified prospects who have expressed genuine interest.

5. Demo Facilitation

Where appropriate, we run structured product demos and walkthroughs with technical and business stakeholders.

6. Qualification & Handoff

We qualify opportunities against your criteria and hand off warm leads to your closing team with full context and notes.

What We Don't Do

  • ❌ We do not claim guaranteed revenue or specific deal volumes.
  • ❌ We do not work with unverified vendors or products without product-market fit evidence.
  • ❌ We do not spam or use non-compliant outreach methods.
  • ❌ We do not represent competing products in the same category simultaneously.
  • ❌ We do not guarantee exclusivity beyond category-level non-compete.
